Where self-reported effectiveness diverges from operational reality — scoring integrity analysis

Methodology: The Confidence-Adjusted Score recalibrates each studio's raw self-assessment by cross-referencing four operational signals: Delivery Status (Yes = 1.0x, Somewhat = 0.7x, No = 0x), Sub-Capability Coverage (% of sub-capabilities delivered), Constraint Load (number of constraints cited, penalised above 3), and Resource Backing (whether FTE allocation is proportionate to the score). Gaps between raw and adjusted scores surface where self-perception outpaces operational maturity — i.e., where leadership should probe deeper before accepting the self-assessment at face value.
